The Process

Our process is simple, transparent, and efficient. See how we bring ideas to life in 3 simple steps:
  1. The machine reads the music note by note, keeping note of the current chord the note is in context.
  2. Constructs a suitable voicing based on the settings and preferences of the user.
  3. Writes the notes into the sheet music.
  4. Returns the modified sheet music back to the user! It is all yours!
